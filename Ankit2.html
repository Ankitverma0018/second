About Our School


Founded in 1985, Northridge Academy has a rich tradition of academic excellence and
character development.


Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and challenging educational environment where students develop into well-rounded individuals equipped with the knowledge, skills, and character to succeed in a rapidly changing world.


Our Values

📖 Academic Excellence
Setting high standards and helping students achieve their fullest potential.

👥 Community
Fostering a sense of belonging, respect, and collaboration.

⚙ Innovation
Embracing creativity and forward-thinking approaches to education.

🥈Integrity
Promoting honesty, ethics, and responsibility in all endeavors.
